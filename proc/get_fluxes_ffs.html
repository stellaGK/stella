Calculate the total particle, momentum and heat fluxes (pflx, vflx, qflx) and the contributions from a given (kx,ky,z) location (pflx_kxkyz, vflx_kxkyz, qflx_kxkyz) inputs are the particle density (dens), parallel flow (upar) and pressure (pres)

assume a single flux annulus

obtain the y-component of the electric field that appears as a factor in the flux expression due to the radial component of the ExB velocity compute the flux surface area = int dy dz (dalpha/dy)*(dpsi/dx) / (B . grad z) compute the flux surface average of |grad x| flxfac is pre-factor in front of fluxes
